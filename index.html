Brief Intro to sound map

Soundmap is a digital game where players travel to specific locations to collect music. Each music drop can include tracks from well-known artists or new ones. Songs have different rarities: common, uncommon, and rare, based on their current streams. There are also three special rarities: Shiny, Epic, and Exclusive songs. Shiny songs can be found through regular drops, collecting gems, or quests. Epic songs are also found in regular drops and rarely through quests, with the first person to get an Epic receiving the #1 version, the second getting the #2, and so on. Exclusive songs are extremely rare and only found through map drops, often during collaborations with artists for limited edition releases. So far, collaborations have included Rex Orange County and Doja Cat. You can also trade songs of any rarity with other players, including up to 8 songs and coins to make a good offer. For instance, if you really want a #1 Michael Jackson Thriller Epic, you might offer 8 different Epics or a mix of Epics and Shinies to make a compelling trade.
