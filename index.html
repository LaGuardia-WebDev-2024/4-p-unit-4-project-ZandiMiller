Brief Intro to sound map

Soundmap is a digital game in which each player travels to various particular locations to collect music. Each drop can include songs from your favorite artist or songs from artists you may not be familiar with. Each song consists of a different rarity ranging from common to uncommon to rare. The rarity of the song is determined by the streams the song is currently receiving. In addition to this rarity ranking, There are 3 other additional special rarities.
